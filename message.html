The theory of you.

A letter for you. For the most wonderful woman that is sometimes grumpy and most of the time very nice. This letter belongs to you.

It all started so fast, so fast that i never really cared about how fast it is anymore. It is a mixture of thrill and happiness, i couldn’t figure out how it all started. As i look back on how it begins, i can only think of what our future would be. You are one of the, if not the best someone i’ve been with. Your eyes that i cannot forget, those lovely voice i always miss. The theory of you, the combination of every little thing that makes you perfect. You reminds me of a non-zero game, you are balanced in all things considered.

Here’s to us, for the future.
